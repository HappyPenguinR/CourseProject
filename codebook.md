##Codebook for TidyData

This dataset has 180 observations and 68 variables. The variables are described in the list below.  
The dataset is organized by Variable 1 (Activity).  
For each Activity, the dataset provides the average mean and standard deviation for each of the 66 measurement variables for each of the 30 subjects.

###Variables 1 and 2

 1  Activity (this variable has 6 observations)  
        WALKING  
        WALKING_UPSTAIRS  
        WALKING_DOWNSTAIRS  
        SITTING  
        STANDING  
        LAYING  
        
 2  Subject (this variable has 30 observations)  
        1-30 for each of the six activities above
